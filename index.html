Pulseway Website Redesign

The Pulseway website redesign was completed under a tight deadline and started with secondary research to better understand user needs and the competitive landscape. The goal was to refresh the visual design while staying aligned with the existing brand. As part of the project, I updated the color scheme and created a clear style guide that defined typography, colors, layout rules, and UI components to ensure consistency across the site. The redesign also improved overall functionality and user experience, bringing the website in line with current design trends, technology standards, and business goals.
