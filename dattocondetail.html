DattoCON-22 Website Redesign

The DattoCON-22 website redesign focused on creating a modern, engaging digital experience that reflected the energy of the event. The goal was to simplify navigation, highlight key event information, and guide users clearly toward registration and agenda details. The updated design improved content structure, visual hierarchy, and responsiveness across desktop and mobile devices. By aligning the site with current branding and user expectations, the new experience made it easier for attendees to explore sessions, speakers, and event updates while supporting overall business goals.
