Pulseway Website Redesign

The Pulseway website redesign focused on introducing a refreshed color theme to create a stronger, more modern visual identity. The updated palette improved contrast, readability, and overall brand consistency across the site. By redefining primary and secondary colors and applying them consistently through UI elements, backgrounds, and calls to action, the new design created a more cohesive and visually engaging experience while maintaining usability and accessibility standards.
